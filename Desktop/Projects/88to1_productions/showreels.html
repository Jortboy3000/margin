Why a Professional Showreel Still Matters

Self tapes are essential, but they’re only one part of the picture. A professionally shot showreel shows casting directors and filmmakers how you actually perform on screen in real lighting, real framing, with real direction.

A strong showreel builds confidence fast. It shows presence, tone, timing and screen awareness, not just how you read lines, but how you live inside a cinematic world. It helps decision-makers see where you belong and trust you in professional productions.

The 88 to 1 Difference

88 to 1 is built by filmmakers, not content factories.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How much does a professional showreel cost? +

Our packages start from $449 for one scene, $799 for two scenes, and $1249 for three scenes — all including shooting, editing, sound mix and colour grade. No hidden costs.

Where are your showreel sessions held? +

We shoot in Perth (May 14–16), Sydney (June 19–22) and Melbourne (June 25–28). Sessions are limited each month — book early to secure your spot.

Do I need to bring my own script? +

No. We provide the scene if you need it. We also run a pre-shoot consultation to make sure the material suits your strengths and the types of roles you're going after.

How long does it take to get my finished showreel? +

Your finished scene — edited, graded and mixed — is typically delivered within 10 business days of your shoot date, ready to upload straight to Showcast, IMDb or anywhere else.

Can I bring a scene partner? +

Yes. You're welcome to bring your own scene partner, or we can match you with another actor from our network at no extra cost.
