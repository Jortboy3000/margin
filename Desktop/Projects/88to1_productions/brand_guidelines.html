1. The Manifesto

"We reject the generic, the flat, and the noisy. Every frame should carry weight."

Core Philosophy

88to1 Productions exists in the space where cinema meets commerce. We believe that a brand's story deserves the same cinematic lens as a feature film.

Pillars

  • Cinematic: Visuals are rich, deep, and intentional.
  • Silence: We use negative space to create tension and focus.
  • Motion: Everything feels alive, fluid, and reactive.

Tone of Voice

2. Visual Identity

2.1 Color System

Our palette is defined by high contrast and deep voids. We avoid flat greys in favor of Cinema Black.

Token Hex Role
p-black #050505 The Infinite Void
p-white #f5f5f5 Primary Ink
s-charcoal #1a1a1a Surface / Cards
a-gold #d4a017 Focus / Accent

2.2 Typography

Primary: Syne (Bold 700)
Usage: Headlines. Tracking: -0.02em (Tight).
Character: Art-deco, geometric, expanded.

Secondary: Inter (Regular 400)
Usage: Body Copy. Tracking: Normal.
Character: Clean, invisible, utilitarian.
